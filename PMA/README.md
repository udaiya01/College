# Predictive Analytics Lab

This folder contains all 11 lab experiments and datasets I did for the Predictive Model Analytics (PMA) subject. 

Did I know what I was doing? Absolutely not. But I had to get those outputs because if my professor didn't see the output, he wouldn't sign the observation. No observation means no record sign, which means zero marks.

**Fun fact:** To even get the signature, we had to show up in full formals, clean-shaven, and wearing a tie. I had never worn a tie in my life before this lab and literally had to buy one just to pass. The codes are here, they work, and my tie-wearing days are documented.
